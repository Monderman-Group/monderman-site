Privacy

How Monderman handles your data.

Diagnostic data stays in your environment. Nothing about how you use the platform leaves it without your action. The rest of this page explains, plainly, what that means in practice — and what Monderman does collect, when, and why.

Last updated: November 2026

Commitments

Four plain-language promises that govern this platform.

  • Diagnostic data stays in your environment. Your responses, scores, exports, and the narrative content of any diagnostic run are processed in your browser and stored locally. They are not transmitted to, written to, or retained on Monderman servers.
  • No sale, no sharing, no third-party advertising. Monderman does not sell, rent, license, or share your data with advertisers, brokers, or any third party for marketing or analytics purposes. There are no advertising trackers on this site.
  • You can ask for what Monderman has on you, and it will be deleted on request. If you have contacted Monderman or signed up for the workspace, you can request a copy of the information held about you, or its deletion, at any time. Both will be honored.
  • Security incidents will be disclosed quickly and honestly. In the unlikely event of a security incident affecting information you have entrusted to Monderman, those affected will be notified directly and promptly, with a plain account of what happened and what is being done about it.

What Monderman Does Not Collect

Diagnostic content, organizational data, and identifying details from diagnostic runs.

When you run a Monderman diagnostic, the platform asks you to describe the conditions inside your organization — its structure, its decision patterns, its administrative load. That content does not leave your browser. Monderman does not see it, log it, transmit it to any server, or hold it for later use. It is yours.

This includes:

  • The free-text descriptions you provide during a diagnostic run.
  • The diagnostic scores, signals, and exports produced from those descriptions.
  • The identity of your organization, your role, or any colleagues you reference.
  • Any document, file, or pasted content used as context inside a diagnostic.

If you choose to export a report or share it with a colleague, that action happens entirely on your device. Monderman has no record that the diagnostic was run.

What Monderman Does Collect

Only what is necessary to operate the platform and respond to you.

Three categories of information are collected, each for a specific purpose, each kept only as long as it is needed.

Contact information you provide. When you submit the Connect form, send an email to connect@monderman.com, or sign up for workspace access, the information you provide — name, role, organization, the content of your message — is received and read by a person. It is used to respond to you and, where appropriate, to support an ongoing relationship. It is not added to any marketing list and it is not shared.

Bot verification on the Connect form. The Connect form uses Cloudflare Turnstile, a privacy-respecting alternative to traditional CAPTCHA, to prevent automated abuse. Turnstile does not use cookies for tracking and does not profile you. Cloudflare's handling of the verification request is governed by their own privacy policy.

Basic site activity. Server logs may record information necessary to operate and secure the site — IP address, user agent string, the page requested, the time of the request. These logs are retained for a short period for security and operational purposes and are not used to build behavioral profiles or to identify individuals. There are no third-party analytics scripts on this site.

Your Rights

What you can ask Monderman to do.

Regardless of where you are based, you have the following rights with respect to any information Monderman holds about you:

  • Ask what is held about you, and receive a plain-language account of it.
  • Request correction of anything inaccurate.
  • Request deletion. This will be honored unless there is a specific legal reason not to (and if so, that reason will be explained to you).
  • Withdraw consent for any ongoing communication or service, at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority if you believe your rights have not been respected.

To exercise any of these rights, write to connect@monderman.com. A response will follow within a reasonable period — generally within thirty days.

Where Data Is Held

Site infrastructure and the limited information passed through it.

The Monderman site is hosted on infrastructure operated by GitHub Pages and supporting services that route requests through Cloudflare. The Connect form submission is processed by a small server function whose only purpose is to deliver the message to a Monderman inbox. None of this infrastructure stores diagnostic data, because no diagnostic data is sent to it.

If Monderman's hosting arrangement changes in a way that affects how your data is handled, this page will be updated and the change will be noted in the "Last updated" line above.

Children

This platform is not intended for use by minors.

Monderman is a B2B platform designed for use by working adults inside institutions. It is not intended for, marketed to, or knowingly used by anyone under the age of eighteen. No data is knowingly collected from minors.

Changes to This Page

How updates will be handled.

If this policy changes in any meaningful way, the change will be reflected here and the "Last updated" date at the top will be revised. Material changes affecting how data is collected, used, or shared will not be applied retroactively to information already held under the previous policy.

Questions about anything on this page, or about how a specific deployment of Monderman would handle your data, are welcome. Reach out at connect@monderman.com.
