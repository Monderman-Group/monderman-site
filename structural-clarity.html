          How this works

          This diagnostic evaluates one structural boundary at a time. It translates signals about ownership, authority, accountability, and governance weight into a directional readout of structural drag and clarity.

          Best use cases

          • Cross-functional teams
          • Program or matrix structures
          • Shared-service functions
          • Oversight and governance groups
          • Recurring ownership disputes between units

          What this is not

          • Not a generic process-improvement tool
          • Not a morale survey
          • Not a personality assessment
          • Not a replacement for deeper structural advisory work

          What to trust

          Trust the structural pattern, not false precision. The strongest value here is surfacing where bureaucracy, governance design, or weak structural clarity are quietly constraining execution—and what that condition may be costing.
