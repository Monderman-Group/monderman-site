Institutional Performance Diagnostic

A leadership-level diagnostic for estimating whether an institution is still converting structure into sustained performance, confidence, and resilience under pressure.

What it surfaces

Execution coherence, performance stability, institutional confidence, adaptive resilience, and reliance on compensatory effort.

What you get back

An Institutional Performance Score, directional benchmark comparison, condition charts, exposure flags, an executive-style summary, and a bridge into deeper advisory review.

Diagnostic Input

Assess one bounded institutional environment.

Use the guided diagnostic for the strongest readout, or upload a template-compatible CSV if you already track institutional-condition or performance-environment signals in a structured way.

Step 2 · Performance condition

This section assesses whether structure is translating into consistent execution and follow-through.

Execution Coherence

How reliably does leadership intent translate into execution?

Priority Clarity

How clearly are priorities understood across the assessed environment?

Decision Reliability

How consistently do people know who decides and who acts?

Follow-through

How effectively does the environment follow through once decisions are made?

This tool is measuring institutional condition, not just operational activity. It should feel broader than the other three diagnostics.

Step 3 · Institutional resilience

This section assesses whether performance holds under pressure, change, and recurring institutional stress.

Adaptive Capacity

How well does the environment maintain performance under changing conditions?

Institutional Confidence

How much confidence do people appear to have in the system they work inside?

Problem Resolution

How often do recurring problems actually get resolved rather than resurfacing?

Performance Stability

How evenly does performance hold across similar teams, units, or situations?

Institutions often look stronger than they are because extra effort is masking weak structural conditions.

      How this works

      This diagnostic evaluates one institutional environment at a time. It translates signals about coherence, resilience, confidence, and compensatory effort into a directional readout of institutional condition.

      Best use cases

      • Functions under sustained pressure
      • Cross-functional operating environments
      • Leadership transitions
      • Post-reorganization assessments
      • Environments relying on visible compensation or heroics

      What this is not

      • Not a process-burden tool
      • Not a decision-speed tool
      • Not a team-structure tool
      • Not a replacement for full performance review

      What to trust

      Trust the institutional condition pattern, not false precision. The strongest value here is showing whether performance still rests on a coherent system or increasingly depends on compensation and extra management lift.
