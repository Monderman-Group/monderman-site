What a seat is, and what it adds: a named login for one person on your team. Each seat can run any diagnostic and see your organization’s full saved history, so the people accountable for each part of the organization work from the same shared picture rather than bottlenecking through one administrator. More seats means more of your leadership on the same instrument. Seats are separate from how much you measure: diagnostics are unlimited on every paid tier, within fair use. At launch we set your team’s seats up for you; self-serve invites — and assigning specific diagnostics to specific people to own — are rolling out.
Fair use. Diagnostics are unlimited — measure as many units, as often, as you need; deploying across the whole organization is the point of the tier. A fair-use standard exists only to prevent abnormal or automated over-use.

Terms, in plain language

These are summary terms, not the binding agreement. They are provided so you know what to expect. Your subscription is governed by the order form or services agreement Monderman provides. We recommend your legal or contracts team review all terms before you sign.
What you are buying
An annual subscription giving up to 15 seats everything in Baseline, deployed organization-wide across every unit you measure, for the subscription term. Longitudinal tracking and dashboard trend views are rolling out. Signal is self-serve: results are interpreted by the software (validated narrative and cross-diagnostic synthesis). Expert human interpretation is the Advisory tier; it is not included here.
Billing & term
$36,000 per year, billed by invoice on a purchase order. Annual term from the start date on your order form.
Seats & diagnostics
Includes up to 15 seats (named logins) and unlimited diagnostics, subject to fair use. Additional seats are available on your order form.
Fair use
Diagnostics are unlimited, subject to a fair-use standard that exists only to prevent abnormal or automated over-use. There is no per-run charge and no run meter. If usage looks automated, Monderman will contact you — access is not cut off without notice.
Support
Priority platform support by email, prioritized ahead of Baseline, plus a one-time onboarding session. Monderman aims to respond promptly; response times are an aim, not a guarantee. Platform support covers using the software — running diagnostics, exports, and access. Interpreting your results is what the Advisory tier is for.
Your data
Your diagnostic inputs and reads are used only to generate and support your diagnostics. Monderman does not sell or share your information, and the assumption set behind every score is published with the read. Diagnostic processing relies on the Monderman platform and its subprocessors; data handling, retention, deletion, and any data-residency requirements are set out in the data processing terms of your agreement and the privacy policy.
Beta status
Monderman is in active beta. It is in production use and improving on a known cadence. Your tier includes a direct feedback channel and hands-on correction where a structural edge case affects your read. You are an early customer of an instrument that is still maturing — not a buyer of frozen software.
Cancellation
Non-renewing by default at the end of the annual term unless otherwise agreed. Renewal terms are stated on your order form.
