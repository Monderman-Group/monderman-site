About

The difference between delivery and drift is measurable.

Monderman exists because almost no one measures it. The platform was built by an operator who has spent two decades inside the kinds of institutions it is designed to serve.

Jason Adamson

Founder

Jason built Monderman after twenty years of doing the work it is now designed to support. He started as an intelligence analyst at NSA, joined CrowdStrike during its growth phase as a senior threat intelligence analyst and analytic support team lead, and currently serves as a senior all-source analyst supporting U.S. Special Operations Command Europe (SOCEUR). Across those roles, the recurring problem has been the same: institutions with serious missions losing capacity to administrative conditions no one is measuring.

He holds a Master of Science in Organization Development from Pepperdine University and a Bachelor of Arts in Russian and Eurasian Studies from the University of Texas at Austin. The frameworks behind Monderman are documented in a forthcoming book from Routledge, but the platform is built for the operator, not the reader.

Administrative reality is not a workflow problem. It is a governing condition — and it can be measured.

Why This Platform

The market measures sentiment. Monderman measures the system.

Most platforms in the organizational health space measure how people feel about their work — engagement scores, culture indices, sentiment trends. That work is not wrong, but it answers a different question than the one institutional leaders have to act on.

Monderman measures the structural conditions that produce those feelings in the first place: where authority is clear and where it is ambiguous, where decisions move quickly and where they stall, where administrative systems accelerate work and where they restrict it. The diagnostics are built to be re-run. Value compounds across the second, third, and tenth read.

Practitioner Principles

Commitments that govern how this platform operates.

  • Diagnostic data stays in your environment. Nothing is stored on Monderman servers.
  • Results are read by humans, not just rendered by software. Synthesis is the work the platform exists to do.
  • No client logo will appear on this site without explicit, written permission.
  • The platform is built to be re-run. Value compounds with longitudinal use, and the architecture reflects that.
