Decision Velocity Diagnostic

A lightweight diagnostic for identifying where decisions slow, why they slow, and where leaders should intervene first.

What it surfaces

Approval burden, handoff drag, ownership ambiguity, rework loops, escalation dependence, and workaround pressure.

What you get back

A Decision Velocity Score, directional benchmark comparison, friction charts, cost estimates, and a narrative interpretation layer.

      How this works

      This MVP evaluates one decision process at a time. It translates a small set of structured signals into a directional readout of decision velocity, friction, and likely intervention points.

      Best use cases

      • Vendor approvals
      • Hiring and staffing decisions
      • Budget and funding reviews
      • Policy or exception approvals
      • Program decisions requiring multiple stakeholders

      What this is not

      • Not an enterprise deployment
      • Not a compliance tool
      • Not a productivity monitor
      • Not a replacement for formal process analysis

      What to trust

      Trust the directional pattern, not false precision. The strongest value here is in surfacing where drag is likely forming and where leadership attention should go first.
