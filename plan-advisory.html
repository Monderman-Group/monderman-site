What a seat is: a named team member with login access to your Monderman workspace — the people on your side who run diagnostics, view results, and manage the account. Seats are separate from how much you can measure; your run allowance governs that and is generous at every tier.

Terms, in plain language

These are summary terms, not the binding agreement. They are provided so you know what to expect. Your subscription is governed by the order form or services agreement Monderman provides. We recommend your legal or contracts team review all terms before you sign.
What you are buying
An annual engagement giving up to 40 seats everything in Signal, unlimited fair-use runs, twelve analyst reviews per year, and a hands-on advisory relationship, scoped to your needs, for the term. Advisory scope, cadence, and any hour commitments are defined on your order form or services agreement.
Billing & term
From $108,000 per year, scoped to the engagement. Billed by invoice on a purchase order or services agreement. Annual or multi-year term as agreed.
Seats & runs
Includes 40 seats (named logins) and unlimited fair-use runs. Twelve analyst reviews per year are included; advisory scope and cadence are defined on your agreement.
Fair-use ceiling
Run volume is unlimited subject to a fair-use standard that exists only to prevent abnormal or automated over-use. Advisory time is defined and bounded on your agreement; it is a personal service with finite capacity, not an unlimited retainer.
Support
Platform support is subsumed by the advisory relationship — you are in regular direct contact. Platform support covers using the software — running diagnostics, exports, and access. Interpreting your results is what analyst review and advisory are for.
Your data
Your diagnostic inputs and reads are used only to generate and support your diagnostics. Monderman does not sell or share your information, and the assumption set behind every score is published with the read. Diagnostic processing relies on the Monderman platform and its subprocessors; data handling, retention, deletion, and any data-residency requirements are set out in the data processing terms of your agreement and the privacy policy.
Beta status
Monderman is in active beta. It is in production use and improving on a known cadence. Your tier includes a direct feedback channel and hands-on correction where a structural edge case affects your read. You are an early customer of an instrument that is still maturing — not a buyer of frozen software.
Cancellation
Governed by your services agreement. Renewal, termination, and notice terms are stated there. No automatic multi-year lock-in unless agreed.
