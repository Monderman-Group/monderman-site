Decision Velocity Snapshot

A guided four-step diagnostic for one decision stream, approval pathway, or recurring operating decision. Complete the structured inputs below to estimate how much delay may be accumulating and what is most likely causing it.

Use note: Do not enter confidential, classified, regulated, or personally sensitive information. This tool provides directional estimates, not audited operational values.

Step 1 — Define the decision flow

Start with one bounded decision stream. Keep it specific enough that one team, approval chain, or operating context could clearly recognize it.

Use the sliders to rate how clear the decision pathway is in practice. Lower clarity increases likely drag.
