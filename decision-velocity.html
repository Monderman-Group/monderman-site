Decision Velocity Snapshot

A guided four-step diagnostic for one decision environment. Complete the structured inputs below to estimate where decision delay is accumulating, how much review burden surrounds the decision, and how much momentum may be lost after a decision is nominally made.

Use note: Do not enter confidential, classified, regulated, or personally sensitive information. This tool provides directional estimates, not audited operational values.

Step 1 — Define the decision environment

Start with one real decision type. Keep it bounded enough that a team, function, or operating slice would clearly recognize it.

Step 2 — Rate the decision conditions

Use the sliders to rate how this decision environment operates in practice. Lower scores generally increase likely delay and drag.

Step 3 — Identify drag patterns

Select the patterns that best describe this decision environment. More drag patterns increase likely delay, review burden, and post-decision drag.

These estimates are directional and based on your selected scope, decision conditions, and drag-pattern inputs. They are intended to make hidden delay and review burden more visible, not provide audited operational measures.
